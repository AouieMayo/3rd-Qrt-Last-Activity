Chocolate Banana

Banana with chocolate is such an irresistible combo that I make this quick dessert often. You can also top these tasty bundles with the butter and brown sugar mixture left over from coating the bananas, or sprinkle on a dash of sea salt.

Ingredients:
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 medium ripe bananas, halved lengthwise
  • 1 sheet frozen puff pastry, thawed
  • 4 ounces semisweet chocolate, melted
  • Vanilla ice cream, optional
Procedure:
  1. Preheat oven to 400Â°.
  2. In a large cast-iron or other heavy skillet, melt butter over medium heat.
  3. Stir in brown sugar until blended.
  4. Add bananas; stir to coat.
  5. Remove from heat; set aside.
  6. Unfold puff pastry; cut into 4 rectangles.
  7. Place a halved banana in center of each square.
  8. Overlap 2 opposite corners of pastry over banana; pinch tightly to seal.
  9. Place on parchment-lined baking sheets.
  10. Bake until golden brown, 20-25 minutes.
  11. Drizzle with chocolate. Serve warm, with ice cream if desired.
