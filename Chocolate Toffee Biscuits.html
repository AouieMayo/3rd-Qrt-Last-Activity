Chocolate Toffee Biscuits

These sweet, crunchy biscuits are so quick and easy to mix up, they’re go-to treat with your morning coffee.

Ingredients:
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 3 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 cup cold butter, cubed
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1/4 cup English toffee bits or almond brickle chips
  • 1 teaspoon butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
Procedure:
  1. In a large bowl, combine the flour, sugar, baking powder and baking soda.
  2. Cut in butter until mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. Stir in milk just until moistened.
  4. Fold in chocolate chips and toffee bits.
  5. Drop by 2 tablespoonfuls 2 in. apart onto ungreased baking sheets.
  6. Brush with melted butter; sprinkle with sugar.
  7. Bake at 425° for 13-15 minutes or until golden brown.
  8. Serve warm.
