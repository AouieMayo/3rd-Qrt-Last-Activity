Nutella Hand Pies

These pint-sized Nutella hand pies made with puff pastry are too good to keep to yourself!

Ingredients:
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 1 sheet frozen puff pastry, thawed
  • 3 tablespoons Nutella
  • 1 to 2 teaspoons grated orange zest
  • 1/3 cup confectioners' sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon orange juice
  • 1/8 teaspoon grated orange zest
  • Additional Nutella, optional
Procedure:
  1. Preheat oven to 400Â°.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk egg with water.
  3. Unfold puff pastry; cut into 9 squares.
  4. Place 1 teaspoon Nutella in center of each; sprinkle with orange zest.
  5. Brush edges of pastry with egg mixture.
  6. Fold 1 corner over filling to form a triangle; press edges to seal.
  7. Transfer to an ungreased baking sheet.
  8. Bake until pastry is golden brown and cooked through, 17-20 minutes.
  9. Cool slightly.
  10. In a small bowl, mix confectioners' sugar, orange juice and orange zest; drizzle over pies.
  11. If desired, warm additional Nutella in a microwave and drizzle over tops.
